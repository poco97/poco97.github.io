Andreea Cristina Tudor

Creative content creator with a thorough understanding of the production process and a keen interest in pre-production as well as post-production. I am seeking an entry level position in any of those roles as part of a diverse, challenging, fast-paced environment where I can meet other like-minded people with whom I can share the passion for creation as well as further develop my skills.
