About Me

BA (Hons) in Film Production and at least 3 years experience in producing creative content (short films, promotional videos, documentaries, etc).

Welcome! I am an ambitious and creative person with a thorough understanding of the film production process and content creation in general. I tend to have a technical and creative approach to matters, combining the two and looking at a problem from different perspectives. Whether being in an office or on the field, I always push myself as well as work with the team to achieve the best results.

I have a good understanding of different filmmaking roles as well as knowledge of equipment and software used within the industry. Having worked on different types of projects throughout the years, I can adapt easily to a given brief or come up with unique ideas. Moreover, I did work experience for a TV programme which helped me get a glimpse of how the media industry is.

The areas I specialise in are: organisation (1st Assistant Director, Producer), camera operating/cinematography and offline editing. I am seeking an entry level position in any of those roles as part of a diverse, challenging and fast-paced environment.
