About Me

BA (Hons) in Film Production and at least 3 years experience in producing creative content (short films, promotional videos, documentaries, etc).

I am an ambitious filmmaker with a profound understanding of the production process and film making in general. I've got a creative and technical approach to matters, always looking at the problem from different perspectives and finding the best solution.

I've got a thorough understanding of important filmmaking roles as well as good knowledge of equipment and software used in the industry. My projects range from promotional videos to short films and I also do photography.

I'm looking for projects to get involved into, work experience to add to my CV and overall meeting other like-minded people with whom I can share the passion for filmmaking. Some exciting personal projects are in the making, with the possibility to be produced during the summer period and the intention to be put into festivals.
