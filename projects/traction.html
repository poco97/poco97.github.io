Traction: The Salford racing society page 

A short documentary following the Salford Racing society as they build from scratch a formula one car. The car will be entered in the 2019 Formula Student competition hosted by the Institution of Mechanical Engineers (IMechE) at Silverstone Circuit.

The project is ongoing, having a couple days left of filming as well as covering the competition happening mid-July.

The current edit of the documentary has been acquired by IMechE to be used for promotional use.

Roles: Editor, Camera Operator (Panasonic Lumix GH5)

Responsibilities: On set I was gathering B-roll footage as well as BTS photos. Due to the scope of the project, I took on board the responsibility of editing it as well as creating snippets to be sued for marketing purposes on social media platforms.

Useful link: https://www.facebook.com/traction.salfordracing/
