Screaming Beast  

Indie Rock Band Screaming Beast needed a music video for one of their singles, Despair. The footage we filmed was used alongside the footage that the band already had. The music video can be found on the band’s social media.

Role: 1st AD, Focus Puller

Responsibilities: I made sure we were on schedule and clapped each shot. Moreover, I was helping the camera operator with focus pulling for more complex shots.
