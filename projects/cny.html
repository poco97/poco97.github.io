Chinese New Year 

The Manchester Chinese community organised the event celebrating culture, tradition and community coming together for the Chinese New ‘year of the dog’. The event took place in MediaCity UK and the footage was used to create a promotional video.

Role: Camera Operator

Responsibilities: Covered each performance, focusing on close-up and side-angles. Being quite a busy event, I made sure not to disturb any of the performances as well as adapt to the fast-paced environment.
